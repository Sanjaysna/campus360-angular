Department Info

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

Computer Science and Engineering focuses on software development, programming, artificial intelligence, and computer systems. Students gain strong technical skills to build innovative software and technology solutions.

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

BCA provides a strong foundation in programming, databases, networking, and web technologies. It prepares students for careers in software development and the IT industry.

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

MCA is a postgraduate program that enhances advanced programming, software engineering, and application development skills. It prepares graduates for leadership roles in the IT sector.

Integrated Master of Computer Applications (IMCA)

IMCA is a five-year integrated program combining undergraduate and postgraduate computer application studies. It offers in-depth knowledge of modern computing technologies and software development.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

EEE focuses on electrical systems, power generation, electronics, and automation technologies. Students learn to design, develop, and maintain modern electrical and electronic systems.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

MBA develops leadership, management, finance, marketing, and strategic decision-making skills. It prepares graduates for managerial and entrepreneurial careers across various industries.
